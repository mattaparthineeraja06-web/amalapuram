Welcome to konaseema , Amalapuram

Amalapuram is a town in Andhra Pradesh known for its ancient temples and vibrant culture.
Konaseema is a scenic delta region of the Godavari River, famous for coconut groves and lush green fields.
The area is a hub for agriculture, festivals, and peaceful rural life, attracting both devotees and tourists.

and significance as the district headquarters for Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Konaseema district

Kalasam

The Amalapuram kalasam is a traditional, often brass or copper,
pot-shaped decorative structure placed atop Hindu temple towers (gopurams) in the Amalapuram region,
symbolizing divinity and the pinnacle of spiritual energy.

clock tower

The Amalapuram Clock Tower was a 40-foot-tall,
square historical landmark with four clocks that served as a central hub and
"heartbeat" for the Konaseema region until it was demolished in 2018 due to weakening and lack of repairs.
