Konaseema Festivals

Celebrating the vibrant culture and traditions of Konaseema

Sankranti

The most celebrated festival in Konaseema, marking the harvest season with joy, rangoli, and traditional foods.

Kanuma

Celebrated after Sankranti, Kanuma honors cattle and showcases rural traditions and community feasts.

Maha Shivaratri

A sacred night dedicated to Lord Shiva, observed with fasting, prayers, and temple celebrations.

Vinayaka Chavithi

Devotees worship Lord Ganesha with devotion, colorful idols, and cultural programs across Konaseema.

Dasara

A grand celebration symbolizing the victory of good over evil, with temple rituals and festivities.

Deepavali

The festival of lights brings happiness, lamps, sweets, and fireworks to every home in Konaseema.
